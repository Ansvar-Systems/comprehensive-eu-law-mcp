# Changelog

All notable changes to this project will be documented in this file.

The format is based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.1.0/),
and this project adheres to [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/spec/v2.0.0.html).

## [0.1.0] - 2026-02-28

### Added

- 13 tools covering EU treaties, regulations, directives, case law, and transposition tracking
- 312KB database: 17 acts, 235 articles, 13 CJEU cases, 10 transposition records
- Treaty of the European Union (TEU) and Treaty on the Functioning of the EU (TFEU)
- Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union
- 8 regulations: GDPR, AI Act, DORA, DMA, DSA, Data Act, CRA, eIDAS 2.0
- 6 directives: NIS2, PSD2, CER, ePrivacy, AI Liability, Product Liability
- 13 CJEU landmark cases including Schrems I/II, Google Spain, Digital Rights Ireland
- NIS2 transposition status tracking across 10 member states
- Full-text search across all articles via FTS5
- Case law search with court and importance filtering
- Legal stance builder aggregating legislation and case law
- Citation validator for articles and case numbers
- Dual transport: stdio (npm) + Streamable HTTP (Vercel)
